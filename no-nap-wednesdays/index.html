A midweek pact

No Nap Wednesdays

Wednesday is the hinge of the week: too far from Monday to be noble, too far from Friday to coast. No Nap Wednesdays is a small, unserious rule for keeping the middle of the week awake, curious, and just productive enough.

N

The pledge

No sofa drift. No accidental duvet admin. No "just ten minutes" that becomes a lost afternoon. Take the walk, make the drink, finish the thing, and let bedtime have the victory it earned.

Approved substitutes

  • The brisk reset Ten minutes outside, preferably with weather that has opinions.
  • The useful brew Tea, coffee, or something cold in a proper glass. Ceremony beats sleepiness.
  • The tiny finish Pick one task small enough to complete before the kettle cools.
